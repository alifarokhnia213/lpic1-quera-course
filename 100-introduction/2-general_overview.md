# A general look into quera's lpic1 course:
**LPIC1** is one of the most popular certificates among companies and tech community, and is established by LPI (Linux Professional Institute) for people who want to show and proof their skills and profession in using linux.


**LPIC1** is a general material and is not specific to any linux distribution, this means it covers all concepts from both **RPM** & **Debian** distributions.
the main and original lpic1 certificate from **LPI** which is well-know across the world can be gained by taking two exams (101 & 102).
